What is a Payment Gateway?

A payment gateway is a solution that allows businesses to process online payments securely. It is a vital bridge connecting customers, merchants, and financial institutions, ensuring the protected transmission of sensitive information during transactions on websites or mobile apps. When customers complete purchases, the payment gateway encrypts confidential details, facilitating a secure transfer to the financial institution for authorisation.

How does a Payment Gateway work?

Payment gateways facilitate worldwide online transactions. After order confirmation, SSL encrypts payment details, which pass through the merchant’s website to the chosen gateway and then to the merchant’s bank. The bank verifies the transaction, and the payment gateway notifies the merchant.

Why is Payment Gateway integration important?

E-commerce can only function with payment gateway integration. The importance of payment gateway integration goes beyond functionality, seamlessly refining the transaction process on websites or mobile applications. Payment gateway integration enhances overall efficiency and strengthens online security, establishing a foundation for trust and reliability in the constantly evolving digital market.
